##  About Hotflame Biogas

**Hotflame Biogas**, located in **Narok Town**, is a leading clean energy initiative specializing in **biogas production and integrated biogas solutions**.

With vast experience in sustainable energy systems, we provide innovative and environmentally friendly solutions that transform organic waste into valuable energy for homes, farms, and institutions.

We believe:

> **"Waste is not waste until it is wasted."**

---

##  What We Do

###  Biogas Plant Installation
We design and install efficient biogas systems tailored for households, farms, schools, and businesses.

###  Appliance Installation & Setup
Professional setup of:
- Biogas cookers
- Gas lamps
- Heating systems
- Mechanical gas-powered applications

###  Restoration of Dormant Systems
We restore and optimize non-functional biogas systems back to productive use.

### Waste Value Addition
Transforming organic waste into:
- Clean cooking gas
- Organic fertilizer
- Renewable energy

###  Civic Education & Community Training
We conduct education programs on:
- Environmental conservation
- Waste management
- Smart climate change mitigation
- Sustainable living practices
- Better home economics
